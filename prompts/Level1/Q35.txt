Dmitry has n cubes. Each cube has a number on it, and Dmitry has a favorite cube at position f.

He arranges the cubes in descending order based on their numbers.
Then, he removes the top k cubes.
You need to determine if his favorite cube will be removed after sorting and removing the cubes.

For each test case, output:

"YES" if his favorite cube will definitely be removed,
"NO" if his favorite cube will definitely not be removed,
"MAYBE" if it's uncertain.
